The List of the Best Records

Band Name Hometown Release Name Format Label Review
Pinch Points Melbourne, Australia Process LP Exploding in Sound Catchy, preachy in the best way, upbeat but quietly aggressive commentary on the zeitgeist and despair of 2022.
BÖRN Reykjavik, Iceland Drottningar Dauðans LP Iron Lung Finally another LP from these Icelandic weirdos. Great dark post-punk.
Neutrals Oakland, CA Bus Stop Nights EP Static Shock “Gary Borthwick Says” is an all time classic; this is the best record yet from an already great band.
Blemish Montréal, Canada Blemish LP Not for the Weak The UK anarcho-punk influence on these Montréalers is obvious but with dual guitars, great melodies, a little bit of artsiness and a feeling of modernity it's much more than just a rehash. This is a vinyl pressing of their 2021 demo.

Crna Žuč Croatia Crna Žuč cassette Doomtown Doomtown Records out of Croatia has been releasing a string of great releases for a while now. This is a solo project by Dragana of Apsurd and has a very gothy, post punk feel that reminds me of a lot of bands that came out of Eastern Europe in the '80s.
girlSperm Different places! The Muse Ascends LP Thrilling Living To be honest I was nervous that this riot grrrl and adjacent supergroup (Tobi from BIKINI KILL, Layla from SKINNED TEEN and Marissa from WET DRAG) would be a letdown but it exceeded my expectations. It has the chaotic and minimalist feeling of the best riot grrrl with surprisingly palpable joy to be playing together.
